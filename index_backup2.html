WELCOME TO CNN.VISUALIZER

This tool lets you look inside MobileNetV2, a real-world AI architecture used in mobile devices, to see exactly how it recognizes images.

HOW TO EXPLORE:

  • Step 1: Upload an image and click ▶ PLAY to watch the data flow.
  • Step 2: Click on any glowing node on the bottom timeline to inspect that specific layer.
  • Step 3: Read the Real-World Analogy and One-Line Intuition in the right panel if the math gets heavy.
Note on Complexity: You will see advanced layers like Depthwise and Bottleneck. Don't panic! These are simply hyper-efficient shortcuts the AI uses to save processing power while acting like a normal convolution.
